About Us

Welcome to GEMACHU TAMIRU COFFEE

Gemachu Tamiru Coffee is a leading grower and exporter of premium-quality coffee, established in 2013 G.C. With a strong commitment to excellence, we specialize in cultivating, processing, and exporting high-quality coffee beans from Ethiopia’s finest coffee-growing regions to international markets. Our dedication to sustainable practices and customer satisfaction has positioned us as a trusted name in the global coffee industry.
